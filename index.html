About Me

I do research in parallel and distributed computing. I have been mostly focusing on ideas related to blockchain technology, where we are developing sound and scalable methodologies for building scalable and efficient smart contract execution algorithms in blockchains. I also research in developing efficient software transactional memory systems (STMs) and concurrent data-structures. Prior to this, I worked on cloud computing, specifically focused on cloud service brokerage system and service provider evaluation using fuzzy rough set techniques.
Currently, I am pursuing Doctorate under the supervision of Prof. Sathya Peri at the Department of CSE of Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IIT Hyderabad). I got my MTech degree in Computer Science with distinction from the School of Computer and Information Sciences (SCIS) at University of Hyderabad (UoH).
